Although many aspiring entrepreneurs start with an idea for a new business, the question remains: Is the idea a business opportunity? Either way, you need to familiarize yourself with different types of business opportunities and learn to identify them. Lessons for recognizing and acting on business and market opportunities can be gleaned from Harvard Business School Professor Clayton Christensen, who teaches the online course Disruptive Strategy.
